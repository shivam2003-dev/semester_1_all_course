## üìñ Essential Textbooks & Papers

### Foundational Papers (Must Read)

#### Linear Algebra & Optimization
- [An Introduction to Statistical Learning](https://www.statlearning.com/) - Accessible ML theory
- [Elements of Statistical Learning](https://hastie.su.domains/ElemStatLearn/) - Comprehensive reference

#### Machine Learning
- [Random Forests - Leo Breiman](https://www.stat.berkeley.edu/~breiman/randomforest2001.pdf)
- [Support Vector Machines](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/svmtutorial.pdf)

#### Deep Learning
- [Attention is All You Need](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) - Transformer paper (Revolutionary)
- [ImageNet Classification with Deep CNNs](https://papers.nips.cc/paper/2012/hash/c399862d3b9d6b76c8436e924a68c45b-Abstract.html) - AlexNet
- [Very Deep Convolutional Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1409.1556) - VGG
- [Residual Learning for Deep Recognition](https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.03385) - ResNet
- [Long Short-Term Memory](https://www.bioinf.jku.at/publications/older/2604.pdf) - LSTM paper
- [BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.04805)
- [Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners](https://d4mucfpksywv.cloudfront.net/better-language-models/language_models_are_unsupervised_multitask_learners.pdf) - GPT-2

#### Data & Databases
- [The Data Warehouse Toolkit - Ralph Kimball](https://www.kimballgroup.com/) - Dimensional modeling bible
- [Fundamentals of Database Systems - Elmasri & Navathe](https://www.pearson.com/)
