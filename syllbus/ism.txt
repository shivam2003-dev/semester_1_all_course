

## Introduction to Statistical Methods (ISM) – Syllabus Overview

### 1. Basics of Statistics & Probability

* Measures of central tendency: mean, median, mode
* Measures of variability: variance, standard deviation
* Data symmetry, skewness, outliers, five-number summary
* Basic probability concepts
* Axioms of probability
* Mutually exclusive and independent events



---

### 2. Conditional Probability & Bayes Theorem

* Conditional probability
* Total probability theorem
* Independent events
* Bayes theorem (with proof)
* Introduction to Naïve Bayes classifier



---

### 3. Random Variables & Probability Distributions

**Random Variables**

* Discrete and continuous random variables
* Joint distributions
* Expectation, mean, variance, covariance
* Transformation of random variables

**Probability Distributions**

* Bernoulli
* Binomial
* Poisson
* Normal (Gaussian)
* Introduction to t, F, and Chi-square distributions

### 4. Sampling & Hypothesis Testing

* Random and stratified sampling
* Sampling distributions

* Point and interval estimation
* Confidence intervals
* Hypothesis testing:

  * Mean-based tests
  * Proportion-based tests
* Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE)
* ANOVA:

  * Single-factor
  * Two-factor



---

### 5. Prediction & Forecasting

**Correlation & Regression**

* Correlation analysis
* Simple linear regression
* Model interpretation and diagnostics

**Time Series Analysis**

* Components of time series
* Moving average and weighted moving average
* AR model
* ARMA, ARIMA
* SARIMA, SARIMAX
* VAR, VARMAX
* Simple exponential smoothing

### 6. Gaussian Mixture Models & EM

* Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM)

* Parameter estimation using EM

### 7. Practical & Lab Components

* Statistical data visualization and summary
